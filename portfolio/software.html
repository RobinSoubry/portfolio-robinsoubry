Home To Horizon

Home To Horizon is a marketplace for sponsorship requests: Influencers can ask sponsoring with a few simple clicks while brands get the tools to measure the return on the partnership.

Pioneer

Pioneer is a native iOS application for travel inspiration & discovery. A user can search for any travel destination in the world and swipe left (dislike) or right (like) for each presented highlight.

Sentimail

Sentimail is a web application, that reduces the pains of writing emails.
The app analyses email drafts on time-to-read, word frequency, formality and tone/sentiment.
