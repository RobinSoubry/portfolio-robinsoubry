Project Description

Sentimail is a web application, soon to be chrome extension that reduces the pains of writing emails. Utilizing the Watson API via a decoupled Rails App, anyone can now instantly analyze their email drafts, rather than wasting hours going back and forth, unsure of the effectiveness of their messages.

  • Time to read An indication of the time to read for the written e-mail.
  • Word frequency Shows you how often you have used the same word in your text.
  • Formality score Indicates how formal you're writing style is and provides suggestions on how to replace specific words by more formal characters.
  • Tone & sentiment analysis Indicates the tone and sentiment of the e-mail, based on IBM Watson's cognitive research.
