Project Description

Explorers often look for financial resources & support. Finding sponsors however is tedious, time-consuming and removes the focus from the essence: Exploring!

Brands on the other hand look for unique & authentic stories to relate themselves to or showcase their products. They lack the proper tools to structure these sponsorship requests, screen the applicants and follow up on the return on investment.

Home To Horizon is a marketplace for sponsorship influencers and brands.

  • Explorers create a detailed profile for their project, link their different social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.) and request sponsorship in an efficient way.
  • Brand Managers receive the requests with key performance indicators on audience size and its fit with the company's policy. He/she can then easily benchmark different projects and grand the fund, gear or other with a few simple clicks.
